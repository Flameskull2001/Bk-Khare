SEBI Updates

Management And Advisory Services By AMCs To Foreign Portfolio Investors (“FPIs”)

The Capital markets regulator SEBI has allowed Asset Management Companies (AMCs) of mutual funds to provide management and advisory services to foreign portfolio investors owned by central banks, sovereign wealth funds, international multilateral organisations and other agencies including entities which are controlled or at least 75 per cent owned by foreign Governments. In September, SEBI had issued norms on classification for FPIs and simplified their registration process as part of ease of doing business in India. View More
